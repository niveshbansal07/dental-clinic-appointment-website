General & Preventive Dentistry

Keep cavities and gum disease away with proactive, painless care.

Our general dentistry services are designed to keep your teeth and gums healthy for life. Every visit includes a thorough oral examination, digital X-rays if required, gentle scaling and polishing, and a personalised hygiene plan. Regular visits every six months dramatically reduce your risk of painful dental emergencies and expensive procedures.

Treatments include fillings, fluoride treatments, sealants, gum care, bite analysis and management of tooth sensitivity. If you are searching for a “family dentist near me”, our warm team and child-friendly space make every visit comfortable.

  • 6-monthly check-ups and cleanings
  • Tooth-coloured fillings and sealants
  • Gum disease screening and treatment
  • Preventive plans for kids and adults
